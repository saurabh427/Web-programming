The Solar System Geography

Explore the planets, moons, and more!

Mercury

Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun. It has a very thin atmosphere and extreme temperatures.

Venus

Venus is often referred to as Earth's twin because of its similar size and structure, but it has a very thick and toxic atmosphere.

Earth

Earth is the only known planet to support life. It has a balanced atmosphere and vast oceans.

Mars

Mars is known as the Red Planet because of its reddish appearance, caused by iron oxide on its surface.

Jupiter

Jupiter is the largest planet in the solar system. It has a giant storm called the Great Red Spot and many moons.

Saturn

Saturn is famous for its stunning rings, made of ice and rock particles. It's the second-largest planet in the solar system.

Uranus

Uranus is unique because it rotates on its side. It has a blue-green color due to the methane in its atmosphere.

Neptune

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and has a deep blue color due to its methane-rich atmosphere.
